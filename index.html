Welcome to Your Alma Mater – The Maharaja Sayajirao University of Baroda

The Maharaja Sayajirao University of Baroda has been a nurturing ground for generations of young minds. If you’ve walked these sacred precincts, you are a proud part of a legacy that spans decades. As an alumnus of this renowned institution, you carry with you not just memories, but also the spirit of MSU — one of dignity, evolution, and excellence.

Whether you studied here years ago or just graduated, your connection with MSU lives on Reconnect, Reminisce and Rediscover. Come home to MSU!

About the Office of Alumni Affairs

The Office of Alumni Affairs & Donor Relations at MSU Baroda plays a central role in building lifelong relationships between the university and its global alumni network. From creating emotional connections to empowering impactful contributions, this office keeps the MSU family together.

Scope & Objectives

1️⃣ Alumni Registration & Database
🔹 Register all graduating students
🔹 Provide permanent alumni email IDs
🔹 Maintain & grow alumni database
🔹 Offer database access when needed

2️⃣ MSU to Alumni Connection
🔹 Act as the university's primary interface to alumni
🔹 Authorize campus facility access for alumni
🔹 Administer the Distinguished Alumnus Award

3️⃣ Alumni Fundraising & Contributions
🔹 Strategize & coordinate alumni fundraising activities
🔹 Ensure deployment & tracking of donations
🔹 Share updates with donors about supported projects

4️⃣ Support for Students
🔹 Distribute alumni-funded scholarships & awards
🔹 Facilitate alumni mentorship for students
🔹 Encourage alumni-funded student travel & projects

5️⃣ Faculty Engagement
🔹 Promote visits & interactions between faculty and alumni
🔹 Enable alumni participation in research & consultancy

6️⃣ Alumni Networking & Events
🔹 Organize reunions & alumni meetups
🔹 Send regular newsletters & alumni communications

7️⃣ Heritage Walks
🔹 Create awareness about MSU’s rich heritage
🔹 Connect students & alumni emotionally to the campus
